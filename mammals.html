Lion

  • Common Name: Lion
  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Habitat: Savannas, grasslands, and open woodlands.
  • Diet: Carnivore; primarily hunts large ungulates.
  • Lifespan: 10 to 14 years in the wild.
  • Behavior: Social animals, living in prides; known for their roaring.

African Elephant

  • Common Name: African Elephant
  • Scientific Name: Loxodonta africana
  • Habitat: Savannas, forests, and deserts across Africa.
  • Diet: Herbivore; feeds on grasses, leaves, fruits, and bark.
  • Lifespan: 60 to 70 years in the wild.
  • Behavior: Highly social; live in matriarchal herds.

Antarctic Blue Whale

  • Common Name: Antarctic Blue Whale
  • Scientific Name: Balaenoptera musculus
  • Habitat: Oceans, primarily in deep waters.
  • Diet: Carnivore; primarily feeds on krill.
  • Length: Up to 100 feet (30 meters); the largest animal on Earth.
  • Lifespan: 70 to 90 years.
  • >Behavior: Solitary or found in small groups; known for their deep vocalizations.

Giraffe

  • Common Name: Giraffe
  • Scientific Name: Giraffa camelopardalis
  • Habitat: Savannas and open woodlands in Africa.
  • Diet: Herbivore; primarily feeds on leaves, fruits, and flowers from trees.
  • Height: Up to 18 feet (5.5 meters).
  • Lifespan: 20 to 25 years in the wild.
  • Behavior: Social animals; often seen in groups called towers.
