  • The American Alligator is a large reptile known for its powerful jaws and freshwater habitats.
  • Scientific Name: Alligator mississippiensis
  • Habitat: Swamps, rivers, and lakes.
  • Diet: Carnivore; feeds on fish, birds, and small mammals.
  • Lifespan: 35-50 years in the wild.
  • Behavior: Known for basking and territorial displays.
